Frequently Asked

Is it really free?

Yes. The core tools are free forever. No sign-up, no watermarks, no limits.

Are my files uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device.

Is there a file size limit?

No hard limit. Very large files (500+ pages) may be slower since processing happens in your browser.

What browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari — any modern browser works.
